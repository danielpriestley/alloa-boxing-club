About us

Establishment

Alloa Boxing Club was established in 1992 by Gibby Turnbull in Alloa, Clackmannshire, Scotland. It has produced a number of Scottish District Champions over the years and continues to produce high quality fighters, trained by certified coaches in our Bowmar Training Centre.

LEGACY

The current head coach John Whyte and his enthusiastic team of coaches aim to expand the club and carry on the legacy left behind by Gibby and mirror his efforts of creating opportunities for the youth and community of Alloa.

REMEMBRANCE

The club would like to honour Gibby for all of the work he done in creating the club we have today. His legacy inspired a generation of Alloa's youth.

Thank you, Gibby.

Gibby Turnbull

1952 - 2013
