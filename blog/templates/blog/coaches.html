COACHES

JohnWhyte

John has been a part of Alloa boxing club since 1997 and had his first bout at 15 years of age. John retired from competitive boxing after 84 bouts to concentrate on coaching. John has an impressive record, winning Scottish, Eastern and School Boy titles during his career. He has now been coaching for over 8 years.

KellyWhalen

Kelly started boxing at the age of 38. Kelly was always into fitness and wanted to give boxing a try. Once she did there was no going back, winning the Scottish Novice title and winning 5 out of her 6 bouts before she hit the maximum age limit. Although she had to retire from competitive fighting she still loved the sport so she decided to get into coaching to help encourage more females to get involved with boxing.

StevieBernard

Stevie has been at the club since 1998. He first started boxing just to keep fit at the age of 28 but soon got the bug for it and went on to have 15 fights winning a silver medal in the Scottish Championships.
